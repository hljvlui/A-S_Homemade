Our Story

We are two ambitious students who shared a dream of bringing authentic homemade flavors to the world by opening our own restaurant. What started as late-night cooking sessions in our dorm has now grown into a cozy space where we serve our favorite recipes. We invite you to join us on this delicious journey and taste the passion we put into every single dish.
