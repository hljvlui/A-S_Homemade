Classic Brownie

Description: Fudgy, dense, and incredibly chocolatey. The ultimate comfort food for chocolate lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 150g butter
  • 200g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g flour
  • 30g cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together. Whisk in sugar and eggs.
  2. Fold in flour and cocoa powder until just combined.
  3. Pour into a square tin and bake at 180°C for 20-25 minutes. Do not overbake!
