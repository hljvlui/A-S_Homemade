Lemon Tart

Description: A refreshing classic with a lemon base. Perfect for summer days or a light dessert.

Ingredients:

  • For the crust: 200g flour, 100g butter, 50g sugar, 1 egg yolk
  • For the filling: 4 lemons (juice and zest), 150g sugar, 4 eggs, 100g butter, 1 tbsp cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Make the crust: Mix flour, sugar, and butter. Add egg yolk to bind. Chill, then bake blind at 180Â°C for 20 mins.
  2. Make the filling: Whisk eggs, sugar, lemon juice, and zest. Cook over low heat until thickened. Stir in butter.
  3. Pour filling into crust and chill for at least 4 hours before serving.
