Our Signature Recipe: Apple Pie

Description: A classic American pie with a flaky crust and spiced apple filling, perfect for any special occasion.

Ingredients:

  • For the crust: 250g flour, 150g cold butter, 50g sugar, 1 egg, cold water
  • For the filling: 6 apples (peeled and sliced), 100g sugar, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, juice of 1 lemon
  • For decoration: Beaten egg for glazing

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust by mixing flour, butter, and sugar until crumbly. Add egg and water to form a dough. Divide into two parts and chill.
  2. For the filling, mix apples with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice.
  3. Roll out one part of the crust into a pie dish, add the filling, cover with the other crust. Brush with egg and bake at 200°C for 40-45 minutes.
