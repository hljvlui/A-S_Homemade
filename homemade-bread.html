Homemade Bread

Description: There's nothing like the smell of freshly baked bread. This simple recipe yields a soft loaf with a crusty exterior.

Ingredients:

  • 500g bread flour
  • 300ml warm water
  • 7g instant yeast
  • 10g salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, yeast, and salt. Add warm water and oil.
  2. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Let rise for 1 hour until doubled.
  3. Shape into a loaf, place in a tin, and let rise again for 30 mins.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 30-35 minutes until hollow when tapped.
